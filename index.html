About us

About Event Trucker we provide a plartform for you to track your evets on time. Events being important we help you track your events through a countdown from current time to the time of your event.

All you need to do is to sign up first if you are a new user to our website to acces the event platform. later you will enter a short description of the event and set the time to begin your countdown.

Most of what we value is your feedback after using our platform. You can input your suggestions for better improvement or for appreciating our work to help you track your events. You can do this through filling the contact form or through social-media pages below this page..
